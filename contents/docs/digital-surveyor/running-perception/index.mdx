# Running Perception

<Note>
  A perception request starts the automated feature extraction process. Follow these steps to configure and submit your request.
</Note>

<Stepper>
  <StepperItem title="Step 1: Create Perception Request">
    Click the green **Create Perception Request** button.  
    <img src="/images/image49.png" alt="Create Perception Request button" />
  </StepperItem>
  <StepperItem title="Step 2: Choose Attribute Template">
    Select an existing template or click **Create new template** to return to the Attribute Templates step.  
    <img src="/images/image40.png" alt="Select or create attribute template" />
  </StepperItem>
  <StepperItem title="Step 3: Select Your Template">
    Locate and click on the desired template from the list.  
    <img src="/images/image53.png" alt="Attribute template selection screen" />
  </StepperItem>
  <StepperItem title="Step 4: Confirm Settings">
    Add or remove layers as needed, then click **Confirm settings** in the bottom right.  
    <img src="/images/image25.png" alt="Confirm perception settings" />
  </StepperItem>
  <StepperItem title="Step 5: Select Area of Interest">
    Use the sky-blue selection icon to draw your area on the map. Your selection will highlight in blue. Click **Submit** when ready.  
    <img src="/images/image5.png" alt="Select area for perception request" />
  </StepperItem>
  <StepperItem title="Step 6: Submit & Notifications">
    Review the confirmation pop-up, optionally add notification emails, and click **Submit**.  
    <img src="/images/image63.png" alt="Perception request confirmation" />
  </StepperItem>
</Stepper>

<Tabs defaultValue="tips">
  <TabsList>
    <TabsTrigger value="tips">Tips</TabsTrigger>
    <TabsTrigger value="notifications">Notifications</TabsTrigger>
  </TabsList>
  <TabsContent value="tips">
    <Note type="tip">
      • Ensure your attribute template only includes features you need to reduce processing time.<br/>
      • You can adjust AOI shape by dragging vertices before submitting.<br/>
      • Check the processing queue to view request status.
    </Note>
  </TabsContent>
  <TabsContent value="notifications">
    <Note>
      Enter multiple emails separated by commas to notify your team when extraction completes. Leaving this blank sends no notifications.
    </Note>
  </TabsContent>
</Tabs>
