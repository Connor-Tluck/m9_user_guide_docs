# Linear Features QA/QC Workflow

<Note>
  Follow these steps to verify and correct linear features like road markings, curbs, and utility lines.
</Note>

<Stepper>
  <StepperItem title="Step 1: 2D Adjustments">
    Complete 2D adjustments in Plan View to ensure connectivity and rough spatial precision before any resampling.  
    <img src="/images/image45.png" alt="Plan view adjustments" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Manual Vertex Adjustments">
    Grab and drag linear vertices to refine line positions.  
    <img src="/images/image41.png" alt="Manual vertex adjustments" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Merge Tool">
    Right-click a line or press “M” to merge split segments of the same feature.  
    <img src="/images/image36.png" alt="Merge tool example" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Context Menu Options">
    Right-click on a line (not vertices) for duplicate, resample, or drape-to-ground actions.  
    <img src="/images/image17.png" alt="Context menu options" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Cross Section Tool">
    Use the cross section icon to inspect specific areas; view appears on the right.  
    <img src="/images/image71.png" alt="Cross section tool" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Auto-Join Tool">
    Use Mass Select > “Merge Lines” to join multiple lines at once. Set deflection angle, gap tolerance, and dedupe threshold.  
    <img src="/images/image10.png" alt="Auto-join tool" />  
    <img src="/images/image65.png" alt="Merge lines parameters" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 2: Resample Centerlines">
    Filter to show only centerlines and EOP; use Mass Select > “Resample Lines” with your interval/tolerance.  
    <img src="/images/image62.png" alt="Resample centerlines" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 3: Resample Curbs">
    Filter to show only curb lines; Mass Select > “Resample Lines” with desired settings.  
    <img src="/images/image18.png" alt="Resample curb lines" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 4: Stencilized Resample">
    Draw a stencil by clicking to place vertices, press Space to finish, then choose uniform/manual/adaptive intervals.  
    <img src="/images/image34.png" alt="Stencilized resample tool" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Stencilized Settings">
    Adjust window width and interval tolerance in the pop-up; hold Shift to snap to existing lines.  
    <img src="/images/image17.png" alt="Stencilized settings" />  
    <img src="/images/image33.png" alt="Shift to snap" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 5: Refine Complex Sections">
    Create smaller stencilized resamples on curb cuts or rounded corners for higher resolution.  
    <img src="/images/image44.png" alt="Refine complex sections" />  
    <img src="/images/image69.png" alt="Trim excess vertices" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 6: Approve Lines">
    Once QA/QC is complete, click “Approve” or press “E” to mark lines as finalized.  
    <img src="/images/image2.png" alt="Approve line" />
  </StepperItem>
</Stepper>
