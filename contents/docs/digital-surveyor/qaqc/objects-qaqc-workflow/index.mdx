# Objects QA/QC Workflow

<Note>
  The following QA/QC workflow lets you adjust, verify, and delete object features extracted in Digital Surveyor.
</Note>

<Stepper>
  <StepperItem title="Step 1: Open Filters">
    Once the dataset loads, click the filter icon at the bottom left to filter by objects, lines, QA state, template layers, perception runs, and sources.  
    <img src="/images/image48.png" alt="Filter icon" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 2: Select Features">
    Hover over the object's filter and choose which extracted features to view.  
    <img src="/images/image34.png" alt="Select feature filter" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Refine with Sort/Search">
    Use “Sort by” or toggle ascending/descending to narrow your search.  
    <img src="/images/image3.png" alt="Sort tools" />  
    <img src="/images/image62.png" alt="Ascending/descending toggle" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 3: Fly to Feature">
    In the **Features** table, click the crosshair icon or click the feature name and press “F” to fly to it.  
    <img src="/images/image13.png" alt="Fly to object" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 4: Inspect & Edit">
    Review the feature’s info panel on the right. Select and drag to move the object. Then use the tools below to adjust before approval or deletion.  
    <img src="/images/image52.png" alt="Feature info panel" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Lock Camera">
    Click the lock icon or press “L” to orbit around the feature.  
    <img src="/images/image55.png" alt="Lock camera" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: View Imagery">
    Click the **See image** icon or press “C” to access the extraction imagery.  
    <img src="/images/image22.png" alt="See image icon" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Adjust Dimensions">
    Drag the dimension nodes to resize the object.  
    <img src="/images/image6.png" alt="Dimension nodes" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Rotate Feature">
    Use the rotation wheel to change orientation; hold “Ctrl” to fix the pivot.  
    <img src="/images/image12.png" alt="Rotation wheel" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: More Actions">
    Right-click the feature for actions like rotate 180°, split horizontally/vertically, and more—see the full guide at  
    <a href="https://docs.mach9.io/docs/Editing-Features/">Editing Features</a>.  
    <img src="/images/image32.png" alt="Right-click actions" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Tip: Cross Section View">
    Use the cross-section tool to verify subattributes from another perspective.  
    <img src="/images/image29.png" alt="Cross section tool" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 5: Approve or Delete">
    Click the green **Approve** or red **Delete** button—or press “E”/“R”—to update the QA state.  
    <img src="/images/image37.png" alt="Approve or delete feature" />
  </StepperItem>

  <StepperItem title="Step 6: Next Feature">
    Click the next crosshair icon or press “+”/“–” to navigate through extracted features.
  </StepperItem>
</Stepper>
